About Me

Hi, I’m Ruchita — a UX designer who loves creating simple and meaningful digital experiences. My design journey began with curiosity about why some products feel easy to use while others don’t.

I believe good design starts with empathy and understanding users’ real needs. I recently designed Savor – A Smart Pantry App, where I worked on the full design process using Figma, from research to high-fidelity prototypes.

I also maintain my own website, CodeCapital.blog, where I explore tech and design topics. I’m always learning new tools and ideas to make designs more intuitive and enjoyable.
